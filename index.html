About

Here at Beau, since 1975, have been hand crafting our Bonbon's with 100% dedication and love. Our rich chocolates come in a wide variety of flavours to suit any and all tastebuds. Lisa Beau, the founder of Beau's Bonbons, started the chocolate buisness after discovering she was lactose intolerant and deciding to create the perfect lactose free recipe known to chocolate history. Come check our small shop on the corner of Bank street and Gilmore.
