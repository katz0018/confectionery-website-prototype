About

Here at Coco's, since 1975, we have been hand crafting our chocolaty delights with 100% dedication and love. Our rich chocolates come in a wide variety of flavours to suit any and all tastebuds. Lisa Coco, the founder of Coco's Chocolatiers, started the chocolate buisness after discovering she was lactose intolerant and decided to create the perfect lactose free recipe known to chocolate history. Come check our small shop on the corner of Bank street and Gilmore.
